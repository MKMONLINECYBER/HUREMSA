About HUREMSA

HUREMSA is an active association composed of Human Resource Management students at Moi University. Our goal is to bridge the gap between academic theory and real-world practice. We strive to prepare our members for the future of work through exposure to industry trends and opportunities.

With over 100 members, we are driven by a collaborative spirit to foster professional growth and excellence.

Our Activities

  • Organizing Career Summits focused on the Future of Work
  • Job Shadowing Programs with industry professionals
  • Workshops and Skill-building Seminars
  • Networking Events and Industry Panels
  • Mentorship Programs with Alumni and Practitioners
  • Team Building and Leadership Retreats
  • Community Outreach and Social Impact Projects
  • Collaboration with other student and professional associations

Our Achievements

  • Successfully hosted 3 Annual Career Summits with over 20 guest speakers
  • Secured internship and attachment opportunities for over 50 members
  • Conducted company visits to leading HR firms in Kenya
  • Held 5 Team Building Retreats to enhance member bonding and leadership
  • Launched a mentorship program connecting students to alumni professionals
